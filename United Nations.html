Why do we celebrate United Nations in School events?

In schools, the celebration of the United Nations (UN) encompasses a multifaceted educational approach that aims to broaden students' perspectives on global affairs. It involves activities, discussions, and lessons that delve into the UN's role in fostering international cooperation, maintaining peace, and promoting human rights. Through this initiative, students gain insights into the complexities of global issues and the significance of collaborative efforts in addressing challenges that transcend national boundaries. Additionally, the celebration of the UN in schools serves as an opportunity to educate students about the organization's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), encouraging them to consider their roles as global citizens committed to creating a more sustainable and equitable future. This two-fold approach not only imparts knowledge about the UN's structure and functions but also instills a sense of responsibility and awareness, fostering a generation of students equipped to navigate and contribute to a complex and interconnected world


What is the importance of celebrating united nations in school events

Celebrating the United Nations in school events is crucial as it promotes global awareness and understanding among students, fostering a sense of interconnectedness and appreciation for diverse cultures. These celebrations contribute to the development of critical thinking skills by engaging students with complex global issues such as human rights, sustainable development, and peacebuilding. By emphasizing civic engagement, cultural exchange, and sustainability, UN-related events encourage students to become informed and active global citizens, inspiring them to address local and global challenges. Additionally, such celebrations build a sense of community within schools, offering networking opportunities and inspiring the next generation of leaders committed to making a positive impact on the world.


Senior High School in San Nicholas III, Bacoor City successfully concluded the celebration of United Nations 2023

Senior High School in San Nicholas III, Bacoor City successfully concluded the celebration of United Nations 2023 through a diverse and multi-cultural pageant on Monday. With the theme "Equality, Freedom, and Justice for All", SHSSN3 basketball court was filled with joy and excitement as students were able to showcase the diversity of nations through wearing the national costume of each countries associated with the UN. The initiator of the UN 2023, namely the Historical Society led by Mr. Joseph Hate, smoothly operated the pageant as well as the other activities organized by the group, which were conducted last week. Darryl Trajano, whom represented the country of Taiwan, and Renee Mae Trazo, whom represented the country of Kenya, were hailed as Mr. and Ms. United Nations 2023 after the pageant. Meanwhile, winners from other activities organized by the H.S. were also recognized and given the momentum through awarding their certificate and medal.
